Welcome to Nirvana Yoga School

Nirvana Yoga School is more than just another school for teacher training; it is an ancient healing art set in the heart of Rishikesh along the sacred river Ganges, and elevated by the spirit of Himalayan whispers. These ancient healing arts are listed under the Yoga Alliance, USA, and we are among the most trusted traditional yoga schools in India.

Our vision is to honestly share the timeless yogic wisdom along with sincerity, care and devotion. From every breath, posture, and chant, we invite you into a life that feels complete, serene, and very much in alignment.

Our courses are authentic and hold true to the ancient yogic disciplines, but also respect the vastly personal journey of every individual. Whether you join us for a 200-hour training, a soulful retreat, or a panchakarma restorative, the focal point remains steady: to provide the space for a meaningful transformation, where yogis learn, grow, and achieve balance.

With a wise and compassionate teacher team, our residential and online programs take very good care of each student. Nirvana Yoga School welcomes seekers from around the globe to walk with us — if your heart leads you to Rishikesh for the real yoga experience, we stand ready to receive you with open arms.

Why Learn Yoga in Rishikesh

There is a sacred rhythm in Rishikesh that cannot be explained but must be felt. It lives in the crispness of the morning air, hums in the silence between the ringing of temple bells, and moves in the gentle flow of the river Ganga. To learn yoga in Rishikesh is to become part of an ancient stream of wisdom, healing, and inner peace.

Sitting in the serene Himalayan foothills, immersed in the spiritual undercurrent of this holy land, your yoga practice transcends the physical. The asanas start to stir something within; the breathing turns from unconscious to a prayer; the meditation deepens into stillness, as if the very mountains are meditating with you.

The energy of this land holds the memory of sages who walked here before us. When you sit beside the Ganga at sunrise or bring your voice to chanting, you find yourself feeling light, clear, and alive. So come — breathe with the mountains, and let Rishikesh remind you of the vast, beautiful peace that lies within you.

As Taught by the Ancient Masters

Learn Real & Authentic Yoga

Yoga is more than a mere practice; it is an honourable inheritance. It did not begin as a form of exercise but as a way of life handed down from one generation to another — heart to heart, in the still woods and along the snowy courses of hills to ashrams.

The earliest traces of this knowledge are preserved in the Rig Veda, one of humanity's oldest sacred texts, created in the language of spirit — Sanskrit. As one of the best yoga centers in Rishikesh, we honour this lineage through Vedic yogic teachings, considered the very source and highest philosophy of yoga rooted in India's spiritual heritage.

You will be engaged in practices that go far beyond physical postures: sacred mantras, yogic philosophy, breathwork, and meditative silence — tracing the footsteps of the ancient sages. We welcome students from all over the globe to return to the roots, and to learn yoga in India as it was meant to be.

Yoga Alliance Certification

Nirvana Yoga School is an RYS 200, RYS 300, and RYS 500 registered yoga school in Rishikesh, certified by Yoga Alliance USA — one of the most respected bodies in yoga education. Our certification lets you teach yoga with confidence anywhere in the world. Here, certifications are more than a paper; you truly become a yogi.
